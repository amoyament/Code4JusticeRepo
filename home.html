Welcome to Re|Con, a non-profit organization dedicated to helping ex-convicts find employment opportunities and build a better future for themselves. Our mission is to bridge the gap between ex-convicts and employers who are willing to offer a second chance. We think that helping ex-convicts will cause crime and poverty rates to decline in our area. Join us in our mission to create a better future for all.
